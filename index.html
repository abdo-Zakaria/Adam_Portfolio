What I've built

Real prototypes, validated simulations, and production-level engineering — not just theory.

Spring 2026
Pneumatic Wind Turbine with Automated Tracking
Engineered a 3-DOF pneumatically actuated wind turbine with real-time wind-direction tracking. Designed solenoid-actuated double-acting cylinders controlled via 5/2 and 5/3 directional valves — the structure fully pivots to face the wind automatically.
▲ FEA-validated structural integrity under peak wind loads. All components modeled in Inventor, fabricated via 3D printing and woodworking.
Arduino UNO Ansys FEA Autodesk Inventor Pneumatics 3D Printing

Spring 2026
Dual-Loop Heat-Powered Refrigeration Cycle
Simulated a dual-loop R-134a refrigeration cycle applying First and Second Law thermodynamic analysis. Generated T-S diagrams and performance maps across 10+ operating conditions to characterize cycle efficiency.
▲ Identified maximum-COP operating set-point while minimizing exergy destruction across all cycle components.
EES Thermodynamic Analysis Exergy Analysis T-S Diagrams

Fall 2025
Air-Powered Vehicle — "The Odyssey"
Co-developed a compressed-air vehicle from scratch. Fabricated stainless-steel pressure tanks using Lamé equations with Python validation. Optimized nozzle geometry for maximum jet exit velocity and programmed an ESP-32 for real-time solenoid actuation.
▲ Structural safety factor above 3 on pressure tanks. Wireless sensor communication and embedded safety logic fully operational.
ESP-32 Python Ansys Autodesk Inventor MATLAB

Fall 2025
Smart Autonomous & Bluetooth-Controlled Car
Developed a four-wheel prototype integrating Bluetooth remote control, accelerometer-based gesture recognition, IR line-tracking, and ultrasonic obstacle avoidance — all in one system with seamless mode-switching logic.
▲ Reliable operation confirmed across all navigation modes through systematic multi-mode testing.
Arduino UNO Embedded C++ SolidWorks Ultrasonic / IR Sensors

Spring 2025
Hydrodynamic Fluid Coupler Prototype
Designed and simulated a hydrodynamic fluid coupling system in SolidWorks, modeling impeller and turbine geometry, analyzing fluid-driven torque transmission, and delivering a functional prototype demonstrating contact-free motion transfer.
▲ Fully functional prototype demonstrating smooth, contact-free torque transmission between input and output shafts.
SolidWorks Fluid Dynamics Simulation Torque Analysis

Hand Drill Reverse Engineering
Reverse-engineered a complete hand-drill assembly from scratch, producing a precise multi-part CAD model covering gears, spindle, casing, assembly constraints, exploded views, and full manufacturability annotations.
▲ Complete digital twin of a commercial drill — a study in mechanical precision, tolerancing, and design intent reconstruction.
